⚡ UnitX — Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 17, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 17, 2026

Summary

UnitX does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All app data (conversion history, favorites, settings) is stored exclusively on your device using the browser's localStorage and is never transmitted to any server.

What data we store

UnitX stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Conversion history (unit values you have converted)
  • Favorites (converter pairs you have bookmarked)
  • App preferences (multi-unit view toggle)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-party services

UnitX fetches live currency exchange rates from open.er-api.com. This request is made directly from your device to their public API. No personal identifiers are included in this request. Please refer to their privacy policy for details.

Permissions

  • Microphone — only requested when you tap the voice input button. Audio is processed on-device via the Web Speech API; no audio is sent to our servers.
  • Internet — used only to fetch live currency rates.
  • Storage — used for local app data (history, favorites, settings) via localStorage.

Analytics & tracking

UnitX contains no analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or tracking pixels of any kind.

Children's privacy

UnitX does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no user accounts, no data submission forms, and no network-connected features beyond currency rate fetching.

Data deletion

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by clearing the app's site data in your browser/device settings, or by using the Clear History and Clear Favorites options within the app's Settings screen.

Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent update.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue at the project repository or contact via GitHub.
